Registration and conference fee

For registration and payment, please click the button at the end of the page.

Everyone who plans to participate in the ISHW2024 needs to register and pay the registration fee. Payment is by credit card only. Also, registration and payment must be made by August 31 (JST).

Registration fee:

Early-bird registration (before July 28th)
Regular*70,000JPY
Reduced (for students, PhD students - Applicants must send a copy of an official student ID or supervisor’s confirmation letter)*55,000JPY
Accompanying person**20,000JPY
Late registration (payment needs to be finished before 31st of August)
Regular*80,000JPY
Reduced (for students, PhD students - Applicants must send a copy of an official student ID or supervisor’s confirmation letter)*55,000JPY
Accompanying person**20,000JPY

*Regular and reduced registration fee covers:

  • Participation in the conference sessions
  • Coffee breaks
  • Conference Dinner
  • Conference bag with Programme Materials

**Accompanying person’s registration fee covers:

  • Coffee breaks
  • Conference Dinner
