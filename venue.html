Venue

International Conference Center Hiroshima, Japan

The workshop will be held in International Conference Center Hiroshima, Japan. You can read more about this place at their webpage, https://www.pcf.city.hiroshima.jp/icch/index-e.html.

How to reach the ISHW venue?

The International Conference Center Hiroshima is easily accessible by public transport, especially Streetcar (Tram). The closest station is "The Atomic Bomb Dome".

The information about the public transport and tickets in Hiroshima is available here:

https://www.mobiry.jp/en/

Conference Dinner

The conference dinner will be held at the Sheraton Grand Hiroshima Hotel (The entrance is directly connected to Hiroshima Station).
